About us

The Story

CIBO FOODS LIMITED is an indigenous agriculture and technology company located in Lagos, Nigeria. Identifying the gap in efficiency of food provision, CIBO FOOD got incorporated in 2019 and has since been working to deliver its pilot product the Chow 24 application an aggregation platform for online ordering and food delivery.

We have also recently launched a Palm Kernel Oil Production plant in Delta State Nigeria, to help create sustainable and economically viable eco system for PKO producers in the state. As an organisation, we are committed to developing scaleable and cost effective food and agribusiness platforms that gradually addresses the problem of food security in Nigeria. We are passionate about delivering excellence through the entire value chain of agriculture and food delivery process
